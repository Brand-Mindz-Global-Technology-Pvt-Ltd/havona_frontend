Home Cookie Policy

This Cookies Policy explains how Havona uses cookies and similar technologies on our website.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help improve your browsing experience and allow us to understand how visitors interact with our website.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality
  • Performance Cookies: Help us analyze website traffic and performance
  • Functional Cookies: Remember user preferences to enhance experience

How Cookies Help Us

Cookies help us to:

  • Understand visitor behavior
  • Improve website speed and usability
  • Provide better service information

Managing Cookies

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party tools (such as analytics services) that place cookies to help us understand website usage. These cookies are governed by respective third-party policies.
